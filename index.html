About us

Adrixus is a new innovation focusing on the reduction of repetitive task in an automated mode reducing the tedious work and increasing the productivity. Working on same work for a longer time reduces the productivity.

We concentrate on learning new things providing individuals to spend more time in improving their skills. We here are researching on everyday activities of corporate individuals and trying to communicate and get acknowleged by their work culture to provide better facility by automated tasks.

We are trying to build service team as well, which will provide web based, mobile based and UX concepts solutions to the clients. Our service division is more focused on client relationship, unique solution and timely commitment. You can fetch some more details from our service website.
